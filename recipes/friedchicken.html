Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken are pieces of chicken that have been seasoned with flour or batter and are fried.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Cayenne Pepper (optional)
  • 3 lb whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. The flour, paprika, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne, and the chicken pieces are to be put together into a resealable plastic bag.
  2. Put chicken pieces on a wire rack over a backing sheet. Keep any leftover flour in the plastic bag, and put the chicken in the fridge for 45 minutes or until the flour looks pasty.
  3. Add 1 1/2 of oil to deep fryer or heavy pot. Heat out to 375 degrees.
  4. Put the chicken pieces back into the bag of flour and coat the flour into the chicken. Preheat oven to 200 degrees.
  5. Put chicken pieces, 2 to 3 at a time, into hot oil in deep fryer or heavy pot. Fry until golden, turning once at 8 to 12 minutes.
  6. Transfer to plate with paper towel on top to drain, and then serve hot.
