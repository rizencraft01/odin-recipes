Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Fried chicken are pieces of chicken that have been seasoned with flour or batter and are fried.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with mixer in a large bowl.
  2. Beat the eggs one at a time, then stire in the vanilla.
  3. Put the baking soda in hot waler. Add it to the batter along with some salt.
  4. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts in the batter
  5. Get spoonfuls of cookie dough and put them 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheats.
  6. Bake in preheated oven until edges are a nice brown for 10 minutes.
  7. Remove cookies from oven and cool on the baking sheets. Transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
