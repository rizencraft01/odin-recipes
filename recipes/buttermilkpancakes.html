Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

Buttermilk pancakes are just regular pancakes with the added buttermilk.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put flour, sugar, baking power, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.
  2. The buttermilk, milk, eggs, and melted butter are beat together in another bowl.
  3. Combine the contents of both the bowls together and mix with a fork or spoon until you get some batter.
  4. Pour the batter from the bowl into a hot, oiled griddle, cooking until the pancake is bubbly on top.
  5. Flip the pancakes using a spatula, and cook until both sides are brown.
